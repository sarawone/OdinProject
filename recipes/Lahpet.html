Lahpet Thoke (Tea Leaf Salad)

Laphet thoke (လက်ဖက်သုတ်), or Burmese fermented tea leaf salad, is a celebrated national dish of Myanmar known for its complex, multi-textured, and umami-packed flavor profile. It is a unique culinary creation where tea is consumed as a food item rather than just a beverage, blending bitter, sour, spicy, and savory

Ingredients

  • Fermented Tea Leaves(Lahpet)
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Shredded Cabbage
  • Green Chilli
  • Fried Yellow split peas
  • lime or lemon

Steps Instructions

  1. Shred Cabbage: Finely shred green(about 2 cups)
  2. Dice Tomato & Garlic: Dice one ripe tomato and 5-6 Garlics
  3. Combine Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the cabbage, tomato, 1/4 cup of the prepared tea leaf dressing, 1/2 cup of the crunchy mix (split peas, peanuts, garlic)
  4. Dress and Massage: Add 1 tbsp fish sauce (or salt), the juice of 1/2 a lime, and more oil if needed. Using your hands (highly recommended), mix and gently massage the dressing into the cabbage to tenderize it.
